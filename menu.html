FairyTail Guild

Fairy Tail (妖精の尻尾フェアリーテイル Fearī Teiru) is the strongest guild in the Kingdom of Fiore. It was originally disbanded in the aftermath of the Guild War against Tartaros, however it was reestablished one year later by many of its more prominent members. The name of "Fairy Tail" represents a sense of adventure for one to be able to discover more about mysteries and fate. Fairy Tail's original location was situated in the Kingdom of Fiore, on the southern coast of the country in Magnolia Town where it was the only guild (until the arrival of Twilight Ogre). It was located in the south of the town not far from the coast, and the guild was situated about 4-5 kilometers up the Magnolia central path. When the core members of Fairy Tail disappeared for 7 years, the remaining members could no longer afford to keep Fairy Tail's first building so it went into foreclosure. After they left it, they moved into a small tavern, that lies on top of a small hill, and made it their base of operations. Though upon arriving back in Magnolia town after their victory at the Grand Magic Games, the guild's first building has since been rebuilt in the center of the city by the people of Magnolia town as a reward for their victory in the Grand Magic Games.

Mavis has very long, wavy, pastel yellow-blonde hair that reaches down to her feet with a small ahoge; large green eyes with no pupils; peachy skin and a slight child-like build. As a consequence of using an incomplete version of a grand Magic when she was thirteen to save Yuri Dreyar, her body was no longer able to grow or mature from that point onwards

Fairy Law (妖精の法律フェアリーロウ Fearī Rō) is a Caster Magic and variation of Law that is regarded as one of the "Three Great Fairy Magics" distinct to the Fairy Tail Guild.

An improved version of Law that when activated, results in the production of a bright light that envelops the area and inflicts massive damage only on whoever the caster perceives as an enemy, leaving friends and bystanders completely unharmed. The judgement of whether someone is friend or foe comes from the user's heart, and they cannot lie to this Magic. Even if the user directly aims it at someone, the target will not be harmed if the caster does not truly see them as an enemy. It is considered one of the most powerful Magics and is one of the rare legendary spells.

Fairy Sphere (妖精の球フェアリースフィア Fearī Sufia) is one of the "Three Great Fairy Magics" distinct to the Fairy Tail Guild.

A Magic which converts Fairy Tail members' bonds and faith into Magic Power. It takes the form of a large sphere with a Fairy Tail mark and a stylized pair of wings emblazoned upon it. The Magic protects the Guild from any evil and is an absolute defense Magic, powerful enough to defend against even time. The sphere itself acts as a stasis chamber, a place in which time stops, thus everyone within the sphere is stuck in a frozen state in which they don't age. It's powerful enough to protect Tenrou Island and everyone on it from Acnologia's Dragon's Roar.

Fairy Glitter (妖精の輝きフェアリーグリッター Fearī Gurittā) is one of the "Three Great Fairy Magics" distinct to the Fairy Tail Guild.

Fairy Glitter is one of the three Great Magics of the Fairy Tail Guild and is sealed within Mavis' grave. According to Bluenote Stinger, it is a Magic that rivals Fairy Law and is a radiance of merciless light that "denies the existence" of nearby foes. It is also an exceedingly complex and unfathomably intricate Magic that collects and concentrates the light of the sun, the moon and the stars.

Eternal Magic: Fairy Heart (永久魔法 妖精の心臓フェアリーハート Towa Mahō: Fearī Hāto), also known as Lumen Histoire (光の神話ルーメン・イストワール Rūmen Isutowāru), is Fairy Tail's greatest secret and a very powerful Magic, said to be superior to the "Three Great Fairy Magics."

Fairy Heart is located in a secret underground room in Fairy Tail's original building. This secret is only known by Fairy Tail's Guild Master and is passed on to the new Master successively. Precht showed it to Makarov when he became Master. Years later, Makarov showed it to Gildarts Clive when he told him that he wanted him to be next Master of Fairy Tail. According to Ivan Dreyar, Fairy Heart is Fairy Tail's darkness, while according to Mavis Vermillion, it is Fairy Tail's light. Later, it is revealed that Fairy Heart is in fact the body of Mavis Vermillion encased in a Lacrima. Makarov refers to it as the "ultimate weapon" of Fairy Tail.
